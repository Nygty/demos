✦ AI Draft — ready to send ⚡ 27 seconds

Dear Andrew, a wine journalist is very welcome here — and Tenuta di Paganico's biodynamic approach is genuinely worth writing about. Our head winemaker Alessio will be delighted to give you a private cellar tour on Friday morning; the estate produces Morellino di Scansano and a Vermentino bianco from the coastal varieties, all certified organic and biodynamic. He will walk you through the vineyard and explain the lunar calendar harvesting cycle. For Pitigliano: it is one of Maremma's most extraordinary places — a medieval Jewish community perched on tufa cliffs with a 16th-century synagogue still in use. We work with a local archaeologist-guide who is superb; she also covers the nearby Etruscan necropolis of Sovana and the tufa gorge paths (the "vie cave"). A full day is needed. Shall I book Alessio for Friday morning and organise the Pitigliano excursion for Saturday? We look forward to welcoming you Thursday. Best regards, Tenuta di Paganico
