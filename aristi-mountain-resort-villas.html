AI Draft — Ready in 25s

Dear Fiona, September is an excellent time for the Voidomatis — the river runs clear and the water temperature is very pleasant (16–18°C). The Voidomatis is considered one of the cleanest rivers in Europe, fed entirely by underground springs from the Vikos Gorge. For a family with a first-time rafter aged 14, we recommend the classic half-day route (grade II–III, suitable from age 12 upwards with a guide): · 09:30 departure from Aristi by vehicle to the river starting point near Kipi · 10:00 — Safety briefing and equipment fitting (buoyancy aids, helmets provided) · 10:30–13:00 — Guided raft through the lower gorge section: spectacular limestone walls, transparent water, several small rapids · No experience required — the certified guides paddle with you Price: approximately €40–45 per person. Minimum age: 12. Your son will love it. Shall I book this for your preferred morning? Warm regards, Concierge Team — Aristi Mountain Resort & Villas

AI Draft — Ready in 30s

Dear Yuki and Mrs Tanaka, Welcome — Zagori in October is exceptional: the chestnuts and oaks turn gold, the light is soft and the villages are wonderfully quiet. For a gentle village walk, we recommend the ancient cobbled kalderimi (mule path) from Aristi to Dilofo — 3 hours return, almost entirely flat, through oak forest and over a restored stone bridge. One of the most beautiful easy walks in northern Greece. No special footwear required, just comfortable shoes. For dinner: we strongly recommend you eat at the Aristi Mountain Resort's own restaurant that evening. Our chef Stavroula prepares exclusively traditional Zagorian recipes using ingredients from the surrounding mountains and gardens — trachanas (fermented wheat soup), slow-cooked goat kleftiko, freshwater trout from the Voidomatis, Zagori mountain greens with feta. This is not adapted for tourists — it is the food the families of Zagori have eaten for centuries. Please let us know your arrival date so we can prepare everything. With warm regards, Concierge Team — Aristi Mountain Resort & Villas
