You've chosen the perfect island for food! Sifnos has been Greece's culinary capital for centuries.

Cooking class: We work with Chef Nikos Poulis, a 4th-generation Sifniot chef who runs classes from his home kitchen in Artemonas (€60/person, 3 hours). Revithada is absolutely on the menu — traditionally slow-cooked overnight in clay pots in the wood oven. We can book for your group directly.
