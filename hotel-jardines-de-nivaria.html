Dear Alistair, you are absolutely right — the waters off Adeje and Los Gigantes are among the best in Europe for resident pilot whales and bottlenose dolphins, with sperm whale sightings not uncommon. I also need to know if there's a pool away from noise. Any guidance appreciated! Beatrice

✦ AI Draft — ready to send ⚡ 38 seconds

Dear Beatrice, ten nights at Jardines de Nivaria — we will make this the restorative holiday you deserve. Let me offer a gentle framework for your stay that you can adapt as you settle in. For the spa: our therapists suggest beginning with the 90-minute deep relaxation ritual on your first full day — it combines warm volcanic stone, argan oil and Canarian rosemary; guests consistently call it transformative. For your special dinner: our restaurant Terraza del Duque has a Michelin-selected tasting menu; we suggest Thursday evening when the kitchen is at its quietest. I will make a note for your reservation. We are so looking forward to welcoming you Friday. Warmly, Jardines de Nivaria Concierge
