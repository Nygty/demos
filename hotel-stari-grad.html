Dear Yasuko,

Lokrum is truly magical — and yes, the peacocks roam completely free. You will love it.

Ferry pier: The Old Town Harbour (Stara Luka) where the Lokrum ferry departs is a 4-minute walk from our hotel through the Old Town walls — one of the most beautiful short walks imaginable.

Ferry schedule & price: Ferries depart every 30–60 minutes from 09:00 to 19:00 (summer schedule). The round-trip ticket is 150 HRK (approximately €20), purchased at the pier. Journey time: 15 minutes.

Solo-friendly: Completely safe and very welcoming for solo travellers. The island is small (can be walked in 2 hours) with clear marked paths, a botanical garden, a saltwater lake for swimming (Mrtvo More — "Dead Sea"), and a small café.

Must-see: The Benedictine monastery (where Richard the Lionheart was said to have sheltered), the Dead Sea lake for a unique swim, the fort at the highest point for views of Dubrovnik's Old Town, and of course — find the peacocks at the monastery garden in the afternoon.
