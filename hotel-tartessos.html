Dear Tom, You could not have chosen a better place to learn — Tarifa's Levante wind, consistent thermal breeze and flat water beaches make it the world's most established kitesurf destination. September is an excellent choice: warm water (23°C), reliable wind (Levante or Poniente most days) and fewer crowds than July-August. For 5-day beginner courses, we work with ION CLUB Tarifa (IKO certified, 5-star rated, 3 min from the hotel by foot). A complete beginner course typically follows this progression: · Day 1–2: Body dragging and kite control (in water, no board) · Day 3–4: Board work and first rides · Day 5: Independent riding + first session alone Everything is provided: kite, bar, lines, harness, wetsuit, board. Bring: swimwear, reef shoes (optional), sunscreen. 5-day group course: approx. €350–400 per person. Private lessons also available at a premium. Dear Beatriz, The Strait of Gibraltar is one of the most important cetacean habitats in Europe — your children are going to be amazed.
