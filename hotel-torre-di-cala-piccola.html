Escursione all'Isola del Giglio
From: james.w@btinternet.com
📧
Email ospite ricevuta

Hello, checking in Saturday (2 couples, 4 adults). We're very keen to visit Giglio island — preferably by private boat rather than the public ferry. Is it possible to arrange this? And what's the best beach on Giglio? We'd like to spend a full day there.

Bozza IA — 30 secondi Pronta all'invio

Dear James,

A private boat to Giglio is absolutely the way to go — it's a 30-minute crossing from our private jetty at Cala Piccola, and arriving independently means you can choose your mooring spot rather than docking at the busy Giglio Porto.

For a group of 4, we recommend a skipper-led motorboat with a small sun deck. A full-day itinerary: morning crossing to the Spiaggia dei Cannelle (the most beautiful beach, fine white sand, crystalline water), lunch at a simple trattoria in Giglio Castello village on the hill, and afternoon snorkelling around the Punta del Fenaio before the return at sunset.

We can have this arranged before Saturday. Would Sunday be the best day for the excursion, to give you Saturday to settle in?

Warmly,
La Concierge · Torre di Cala Piccola

Chère Anne,

Tout à fait possible — c'est la meilleure façon de découvrir les criques de l'Argentario à votre rythme !
