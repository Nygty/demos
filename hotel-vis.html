AI Draft — 30 seconds Ready to send

Dear Adam,

Welcome — great question to sort out in advance, the ferry schedule is key for reaching Vis.

The Jadrolinija ferry Split–Vis runs twice daily in summer; the afternoon sailing typically departs Split at 17:15, arriving Vis Town at ~19:15. If your flight lands at 16:00 and you clear baggage by 16:30, it is feasible but tight depending on traffic to the Split ferry terminal (around 20 min).

If you miss the last sailing, the best option is an overnight stay in Split (we can recommend a good hotel near the terminal) and the morning ferry the next day — we'll arrange your airport pick-up and a welcome here on Saturday morning. No need to rush with heavy luggage.

Can you confirm your exact landing time? We'll map out the best plan for you.
