Dear Mark and Julia, great choices — and yes, both are very doable in one day from Monopoli. We suggest the following: leave early (8:15 am) for Alberobello — the trulli district of Rione Monti is magical before 9:30 am when tour buses arrive. Spend 2 hours there. Then drive to Polignano a Mare (30 minutes) for lunch — arrive by noon and eat at Ristorante Grotta Palazzese if you can book, or Da Tuccino for simpler excellent fish. The famous sea cave views from the cliffs are best in the afternoon light. You'll be back at the masseria by 5:30 pm for a swim before dinner. We can arrange a driver for the full day if you prefer not to drive — highly recommended, Puglia's roads are narrow in places. Shall I look into the car service? Looking forward to welcoming you Friday! Masseria Montenapoleone
