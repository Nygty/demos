Dear James,

Welcome to Syros — a hidden gem even among those who know Greece well.

Apollo Theatre: One of Greece's finest neoclassical theatres, modelled on La Scala. Guided tours run Tue–Sat at 10:00 (€5). The summer season sometimes includes concerts and opera — we'll check the August programme and reserve seats if anything is on during your stay.

Ano Syros: The medieval Catholic quarter perched above the port is extraordinary. The Church of San Giorgio and the panoramic view from the top are not to be missed. We recommend the morning walk (cool and photogenic) — about 45 minutes from Omiros on foot via the marble-paved stairways.

Suggested itinerary:
Day 1 — Apollo Theatre tour + Vaporia neoclassical mansions + Aegli Square for evening coffee
Day 2 — Ano Syros morning + afternoon Kini Beach
Day 3 — Day trip to Paros or Mykonos (30 min by ferry)
Day 4 — Market district + Galissas sunset

We'll prepare a printed map for your arrival.
