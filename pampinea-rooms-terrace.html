AI Draft — ready in 30s ✓ Draft ready

Dear Lena,

You've chosen the perfect base — our terrace has one of the finest unobstructed views of Manarola's famous clifftop houses and sea in the Cinque Terre.

Golden hour (August): Sunrise ~06:15, sunset ~20:30 (golden light begins ~19:45). For the iconic Manarola shot — the coloured houses cascading down to the sea — the Punta Bonfiglio viewpoint is 5 minutes on foot from Pampinea (we'll mark it on your map). Best light: 30 minutes before sunset for warm front-lit colours, or dawn for misty soft light and empty streets.

Via dell'Amore: Good news — as of June 2024 the path has partially reopened (Riomaggiore side, first section). The full path to Manarola remains closed for restoration works. We recommend confirming the current status on arrival as reopening is gradual.

Alternative cliff path: The Manarola → Corniglia trail (CAI 586, 45 min) offers stunning sea-cliff photography and is fully open. A significantly more dramatic walk than Via dell'Amore.

We'll prepare your room with an early check-in so you're ready for the sunset.
