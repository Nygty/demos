Dear Nils, Great news — both Lipsi and Leros are very accessible from Skala and make excellent day trips in summer. Lipsi: The Blue Star or Dodekanisos Seaways ferry departs Skala at approximately 08:30 and reaches Lipsi in 40 minutes. The island has only 800 permanent residents, a single port village, turquoise bays (Platis Gialos is exceptional) and a handful of authentic tavernas. The return ferry leaves Lipsi around 17:30, back in Skala before 18:30. Perfect as a solo day trip. Leros: Slightly further (1 hour 30 minutes by fast ferry), Leros is fascinating — a large Italian Art Deco harbour town left from the Italian occupation, dramatic hilltop castle, excellent fish restaurants. I recommend staying for a full day; the 08:00 departure and 18:00 return works well. I will print you a current ferry timetable on arrival. Summer schedules change weekly, so I always check before your trip. Would you like me to put together a day-by-day suggestion for your 8 nights? With pleasure, Petros Hotel — Skala, Patmos
