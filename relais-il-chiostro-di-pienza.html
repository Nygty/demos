Dear David and Anna, Those photos don't do it justice — the Val d'Orcia at dawn from a balloon, with the cypress lines and Montalcino in the distance, is genuinely one of the most beautiful things you can do in Tuscany. We work with Ballooning in Tuscany, who are based 20 minutes from Il Chiostro. A shared flight for two is approx. €280 per person; a private basket for two is approx. €700. Both options are wonderful — the private flight allows more flexibility in the flight path. For weather: May mornings are generally excellent. The operator monitors forecasts daily and will give you 48h notice of the go/no-go decision.
