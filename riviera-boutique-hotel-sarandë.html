AI Draft — 30 seconds Ready to send

Dear David,

Yes — that's one of Sarandë's magical details! Corfu is literally 45 minutes by fast ferry across the Ionian (the channel is only 12 km wide at this point).

The Ionian Seaways ferry runs daily between Sarandë and Corfu Town. A typical day trip: depart Sarandë 08:00, arrive Corfu Town 08:45, spend the morning in the UNESCO-listed Old Town (Liston arcade, Old Fortress, Byzantine Museum), lunch at a traditional Corfiot taverna, afternoon stroll through the Venetian lanes, return ferry around 18:30.

We can book your round-trip tickets in advance — just let us know your preferred date and how many people. We'll also give you a short printed guide to Corfu Town's best stops.

Enjoy the view from the rooftop in the meantime!
