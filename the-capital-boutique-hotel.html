Dear team,

This is our first visit to Malta. We'll have 3 full days in Valletta — could your concierge recommend the top highlights we shouldn't miss? We enjoy art, history, and good local food. Not big fans of huge tourist crowds. Thank you!

Tomáš

Dear Tomáš,

Welcome to your first Malta adventure — three days in Valletta with your interests gives us a great deal to work with, and we'll steer you well clear of the queues.

For art and history: St John's Co-Cathedral first thing in the morning (Caravaggio's The Beheading of St John is worth the entire trip), then the National Museum of Fine Arts and the quiet baroque streets of Merchant Street. For something less visited, the Manoel Theatre — Europe's third-oldest working theatre — offers tours.

For local food without the crowds: Rubino on Old Bakery Street for traditional Maltese cuisine in a genuinely local setting, or a pastizzi breakfast at Crystal Palace Bar in Rabat if you venture outside the walls.

We'll prepare a personalised day-by-day map for you at check-in. Looking forward to welcoming you!

With warmth,
