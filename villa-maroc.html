Buongiorno,

My wife and I will be staying with you in November. Could you tell us about the hammam and spa services available? We're interested in traditional treatments — ideally a full hammam ritual. Is it necessary to book in advance?

Giovanni

Dear Giovanni,

What a perfect choice for a November escape — the hammam at Villa Maroc is one of the most authentic experiences Essaouira has to offer, and we'd love to arrange it for you both.

We offer a traditional Moroccan hammam ritual including steam, black soap (savon beldi), kessa exfoliation, and a rhassoul clay mask — approximately 90 minutes in total. We can follow it with an argan oil massage if you'd like to complete the experience. A private hammam session for two can be arranged at a time reserved just for you.

Given availability, we recommend booking a day or two in advance. When would you like to schedule it during your stay? I'll take care of the reservation immediately.

Warm regards,

Objet : Surf spots near Essaouira — your suggestions?
Hi Villa Maroc,

We're arriving next week and we are keen surfers. Could you recommend the best surf spots near Essaouira? Also, is it easy to rent boards locally? We're intermediate level. Many thanks!

Sarah

Dear Sarah,

Great — you've come to the right place! Essaouira is one of Morocco's finest surf destinations, with consistent Atlantic swells and wide beaches a short distance from the medina.

For intermediate surfers, we particularly recommend Sidi Kaouki beach (about 27 km south) — excellent right-hand breaks and far less crowded than the main beach. Closer to the hotel, Essaouira Beach itself has a lively surf scene with reliable waves and several reputable board rental and lesson shops we can recommend directly.

I'll have a map with our specific tips ready for you at check-in, along with a contact for a trusted local surf guide if you'd like a session with someone who knows the best breaks. See you soon!

Warmly,
[Concierge Team]
Villa Maroc | Essaouira, Morocco
