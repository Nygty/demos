Subject: Lavender fields — are they visible in July?
Hello,

We've heard Hvar is known for lavender. We arrive in mid-July — will the lavender still be flowering? And where is the best spot to see it? Any local tips welcome.

Robert

Dear Robert,

Hvar's lavender is one of its great treasures, and mid-July is right at the edge of the flowering season — the fields are usually at their peak in late June and the first weeks of July, so you may catch the very end of the bloom or find the harvest just beginning, which is beautiful in its own way.

The best spot is the interior of the island, particularly around the village of Velo Grablje and the Stari Grad Plain — both of which are worth visiting for their landscape and history regardless of the lavender. The ride across the island by scooter (rentable in the Old Town) is half the pleasure.

I'll have a printed local map with our recommendations waiting at check-in. Let us know if you'd like us to arrange a scooter for you — it books up quickly in July.

See you soon,
[Villa Nora Team]
